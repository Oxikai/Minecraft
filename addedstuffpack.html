Features

Items

  • Ender Gem: a new drop from endermen which let you teleport instantly to a random place or craft ender armor
  • Hopper Boots: boots that let items be siphoned from a player's inventory by a hopper
  • Ender Armor: armor crafted from ender gems, when wearing the whole set makes the player teleport like an enderman when damaged (by player only)
